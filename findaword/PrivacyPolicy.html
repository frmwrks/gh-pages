FindAWord Privacy Policy...

NOTE: This is a draft and has not been finalized.

Effective date: August 31, 2019

frmwrks, LLC (“us”, “we”, or “our”) operates the FindAWord iOS mobile application (hereinafter referred to as “FindAWord”).

This page informs you of our privacy policy regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of any personal data when you use our FindAWord iOS application and the choices you have associated with the collection of that personal data.

We use your data to provide and improve the FindAWord application. By using FindAWord, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Definitions

  • FindAWord: FindAWord is the FindAWord iOS mobile application created and monitored by frmwrks, LLC.
  • Personal Data: Personal Data means data about a living individual who can be specifically identified from that data (or from that data and other information either in our possession or likely to come into our possession).
  • Usage Data: Usage Data is data generated and collected automatically by the use of FindAWord.
  • Cookies: Cookies are small files stored on your device (computer or mobile device).

Information Collection and Use

frmwrks, LLC collects only anonymous usage data for various purposes to provide and improve the FindAWord iOS application for our FindAWord users.

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

  • No personally identifiable data is collected.
  • No cookies with personally identifiable data is collected.

To summarize, no personally identifiable data is collected.

Usage Data

When you use the FindAWord application on your iOS device, we collect certain information automatically, none of which contains any personally identifiable data.

The kind of usage data collected will help us know what areas of the FindAWord application may need improvement, which parts are popular, and which parts are not used often. Some of the non-personal data collected includes:

  • The kind of iOS device being used.
  • The type of a word being researched.
  • The length of a word being researched.
  • Any word that you add to or remove from the application's list of words.
  • Which application options are modified.
  • Which informational pages of the application are viewed.
  • Data about any app crashes that occur.

Other usage statistics may be collected but none of that information will contain any personally identifiable data.

This anonymous usage information is recorded via Google services such as Firebase and Fabric,

User Feedback

The Send Feedback option will allow you to send in to frmwrks any suggestions you may have about the FindAWord application. No personal information will be recorded when such feedback is sent. If you include any personal information (such as your name, an email address or a telephone number) in the feedback, that will not be captured or recorded by frmwrks other than being present in the text which you send. frmwrks will not share that information and will only use that information to respond to a specific request you make for additional communications.

Use of Data

FindAWord uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To improve and maintain FindAWord.
  • To monitor the usage of FindAWord's various features.
  • To detect and address technical issues such as crashes or missing words.

Transfer Of Data

Since no personally identifiable data is collected by the FindAWord iOS application, no personally identifiable data will be retained by or transferred to any computer located within or outside of your state, province, country or other governmental jurisdiction.

If you are located outside United States and choose to provide information to us via the feedback option, that information will retained in the United States and will not be published or shared in any manner.

Your consent to this Privacy Policy followed by your submission of such information represents your agreement to that transfer.

FindAWord will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that data you voluntarily share via feedback is treated securely and in accordance with this Privacy Policy and no transfer of your personal information will take place to an organization or a country unless there are adequate controls in place including the security of your personal information.

Disclosure Of Data

Legal Requirements

FindAWord may disclose your Personal Data in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to:

  • To comply with a legal obligation.
  • To protect and defend the rights or property of FindAWord.
  • To prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing in connection with FindAWord.
  • To protect the personal safety of users of FindAWord or the public.
  • To protect against legal liability.

As an European citizen, under GDPR, you have certain individual rights. You can learn more about these guides in the GDPR Guide.

Security of Data

The security of any voluntarily provided personal information via feedback is important to us but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

FindAWord Providers

We may employ third party companies and individuals to facilitate our FindAWord ("Service Providers"), to provide FindAWord on our behalf, to perform FindAWord-related services or to assist us in analyzing how our FindAWord is used.

Since no personal data is recorded by FindAWord, these third parties will not have access to your Personal Data and so will not be able to disclose or use it for any purpose.

Links to Other Sites

Our FindAWord may contain links to other sites that are not operated by us. If you click a third party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit.

We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies or practices of any third party sites or services.

Children's Privacy

Since FindAWord does not record any personal data, it does not place anyone under the age of 18 ("Children") at risk.

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 18. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your Child has provided us with Personal Data via the feedback option, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected Personal Data from children without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Changing our Privacy Policy will require an application update at the Apple Store and you will be made aware of any Privacy Policy modifications via a FindAWord update notice from the Apple Store.

Should there be any change to our Privacy Policy, we will post a prominent notice on our website prior to the change becoming effective and update the "effective date" at the top of this Privacy Policy.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
