What is FindAWord...

Okay, it’s a cheater app – but we’ll call it a solution assistant. Sometimes, when you are playing a game where you are given a bunch of letters and have to figure out one or two words, things get tough and frustrating. How about an example:

  1. Given: QHUSAOVKOTTRIRNLUT

  2. Find: One word 8 letters long

  3. •Figure it out? Could be tough. The FindAWord app has a dictionary with over 140,000 possible words and, given those letters above, there are 69 possible one-word answers.

  4. •Fortunately, FindAWord ranks the possible answers by frequency, which should make figuring the proper solution easier.

  5. •In this case, there are 2 Frequent one-word possibilities found by FindAWord. (There are a few more in the Common (7), Occasional (9), Infrequent (12) and Rare (39) frequency categories.)

  6. •Also, fortunately, FindAWord, by default, does not show you full word. In this case, it would show you (for Frequent):

  7. R…N, S…N

  8. •A small + can be pressed to reveal more letters. After a couple of presses, you would see:

  9. ROT…N, SOL…N

  10. •And, finally, after hitting the + enough times (or changing your preferences so that the full words are initially shown) you will see:

  11. ROTATION, SOLUTION

  12. •At this point, picking the right, uh, SOLUTION, is easy!

  13. •There are several other helpful options available in FindAWord so be sure to take a look at the Guidelines web page.
