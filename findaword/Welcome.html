What is FindAWord...P

Okay, it’s a cheater app – but we’ll call it a solution assistant. Sometimes, when you are playing a game where you are given a bunch of letters and have to figure out one or two words, things get tough and frustrating. How about an example:

  • Figure it out? Could be tough. The FindAWord app has a dictionary with over 140,000 possible words and, given those letters above, there are 69 possible one-word answers.
  • Fortunately, FindAWord ranks the possible answers by frequency, which should make figuring the proper solution easier.
  • In this case, there are 2 Frequent one-word possibilities found by FindAWord. (There are a few more in the Common (7), Occasional (9), Infrequent (12) and Rare (39) frequency categories.)
  • Also, fortunately, FindAWord, by default, does not show you full word. In this case, it would show you (for Frequent):

R…N, S…N

  • A small + can be pressed to reveal more letters. After a couple of presses, you would see:

ROT…N, SOL…N

  • And, finally, after hitting the + enough times (or changing your preferences so that the full words are initially shown) you will see:

ROTATION, SOLUTION

  • At this point, picking the right, uh, SOLUTION, is easy!
  • There are several other helpful options available in FindAWord so be sure to take a look at the Guidelines web page.
